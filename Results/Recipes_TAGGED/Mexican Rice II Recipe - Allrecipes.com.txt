<ingredients><ingredient amount="3.0 Tablespoon">Vegetable Oil</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Cup">Grain</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Teaspoon">Garlic</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.25 Cup">Onion</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.5 Cup">Tomato Sauce</ingredient><ingredient amount="2.0 Cup">Chicken</ingredient></ingredients>
<instructions>
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat and add rice. Cook, stirring constantly, until puffed and golden. While rice is cooking, sprinkle with salt and cumin.

Stir in onions and cook until tender. Stir in tomato sauce and chicken broth; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
