<ingredients><ingredient amount="3.0 Tablespoon">Olive Oil</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.5">Onion</ingredient><ingredient amount="2.0">Garlic</ingredient><ingredient amount="4.0 Teaspoon">Bouillon</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.5 Teaspoon">Pepper</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.5">Cabbage</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Ounce">Tomato</ingredient></ingredients>
<instructions>
In a large stockpot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Stir in onion and garlic; cook until onion is transparent, about 5 minutes.

Stir in water, bouillon, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then stir in cabbage. Simmer until cabbage wilts, about 10 minutes.

Stir in tomatoes. Return to a boil, then simmer 15 to 30 minutes, stirring often.
