<ingredients><ingredient amount="1.0 Ounce">Pasta</ingredient><ingredient amount="2.0 Tablespoon">Olive Oil</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.25 Cup">Red Onion</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Tablespoon">Garlic</ingredient><ingredient amount="2.0 Ounce">Tomato</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Cup">Parmesan Cheese</ingredient></ingredients>
<instructions>
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in onion and garlic, and cook until onion is tender. Mix in wine and tomatoes, and continue cooking 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Mix shrimp into the skillet, and cook 5 minutes, or until opaque. Toss with pasta and top with Parmesan cheese to serve.




</instructions>
