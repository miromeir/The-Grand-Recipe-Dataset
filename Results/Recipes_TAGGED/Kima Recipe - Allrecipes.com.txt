<ingredients><ingredient amount="3.0 Tablespoon">Butter</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Cup">Onion</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0">Garlic</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Pound">Beef</ingredient><ingredient amount="2.0">Tomato</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Cup">Pea</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Tablespoon">Curry Powder</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Teaspoon">Paprika</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.5 Teaspoon">Chili Powder</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0">Pepper</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.25 Cup">Coconut</ingredient></ingredients>
<instructions>
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. stir in onion and garlic; cook until onion is translucent, 3 to 5 minutes.

Stir beef into onion mixture; cook until crumbly and no longer pink, 5 to 10 minutes. Drain excess fat from beef mixture.

Stir tomatoes, peas, curry powder, salt, paprika, chili powder, and black pepper into beef mixture; cook until thickened, about 20 minutes. Garnish with coconut.
