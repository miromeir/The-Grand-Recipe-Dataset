<ingredients><ingredient amount="1.0 Tablespoon">Olive Oil</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.5 Teaspoon">Paprika</ingredient><ingredient amount="8.0 Quart">Sweet Potato</ingredient></ingredients>
<instructions>
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking sheet.

In a large bowl, mix olive oil and paprika. Add potato sticks, and stir by hand to coat. Place on the prepared baking sheet.

Bake 40 minutes in the preheated oven.
