<ingredients><ingredient amount="8.0 Ounce">Green Bean</ingredient><ingredient amount="2.0 Tablespoon">Soy Sauce</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.5 Tablespoon">Paste</ingredient><ingredient amount="0.5 Teaspoon">Pepper</ingredient><ingredient amount="4.0">Garlic</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Teaspoon">Ginger</ingredient><ingredient amount="1.0 Tablespoon">Sesame Seed</ingredient></ingredients>
<instructions>
Place the green beans into a steamer insert and set in a pot over one inch of water. Bring to a boil, cover and steam for 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and transfer beans to a serving bowl.

Meanwhile, in a small bowl, stir together the soy sauce, miso paste, red pepper flakes, garlic and ginger. Pour over the green beans and toss to coat. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
